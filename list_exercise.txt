Basic List Exercises

Create a list of 5 favorite fruits and print it.

Add a new fruit to the end of the list.

Insert a fruit at the second position of the list.

Remove the last fruit from the list.

Remove a specific fruit by name from the list.

Print the length of the list.

Print the first, middle, and last items from the list.

Reverse the list.

Sort the list alphabetically.

Make a copy of the list and modify the copy without affecting the original.
